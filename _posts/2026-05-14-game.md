**Weave the Elements, Shape your Destiny!**

![banner](../../../../images/posts/2026/05/game/banner.png)


*Spellweaver: The Element Mage* is a 2D action-roguelike game where players combine elemental fragments in real time to forge spells. Set in a decaying underground civilization, you must fight through enemies corrupted by the old world's mistakes and decide whether to preserve a broken order or risk everything for renewal.

![characters](../../../../images/posts/2026/05/game/characters.png)

The game is developing using Unity and C#. Thanks to MCPs and AI...
