[[openreview]](https://openreview.net/forum?id=sjvxLHuCc2): This is a short-time voluntary RA experience with Prof. [Wenhao Li](https://ewanlee.weebly.com/) at Tongji University. The work lasts from Jan. 2026 to Mar. 2026. In this paper, the temporal and structual credit assignment problems in reinforcement learning are adopted to LLM-based multi-agent systems prompt optimization. Text gradient method is applied during a debate process. The idea was provided by the professor. I wrote most parts of the paper, drew some figures and tables, and ran all the experiments. The paper is under review by UAI.
